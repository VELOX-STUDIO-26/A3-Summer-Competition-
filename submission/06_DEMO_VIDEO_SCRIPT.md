## Recording Tips

1. **Screen Recording**: Use OBS Studio or similar (1080p, 30fps)
2. **Audio**: Use a quality microphone; record in a quiet room
3. **Narration**: Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
4. **Transitions**: Use simple cuts; avoid fancy transitions
5. **Captions**: Add subtitles for accessibility
6. **Compression**: Export as H.264 MP4, target bitrate 5-8 Mbps
